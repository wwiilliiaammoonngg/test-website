The Rolling Stones

Some of my favorite songs by The Rolling Stones include Jumpin' Jack Flash, and Gimme Shelter.

Jumpin' Jack Flash

Song was released in 1964, and to this date is their most performed song, having been performed by the group over 1,100 times.

Gimme Shelter

The song was recorded and published in 1969, an was written by Mick Jagger and Keith Richards.
